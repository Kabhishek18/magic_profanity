## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of this project seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability within magic_profanity, please do not open a public issue. Instead, please follow the steps below:
Email the Maintainer: Send a detailed report to the security contact at kabhishek18.com or via the contact methods listed on the maintainer's profile.

## Details to Include:
A description of the vulnerability.

Potential impact: Steps to reproduce (a proof-of-concept script is highly appreciated).
Response Time: You can expect an acknowledgment within 48-72 hours. We will keep you updated on the progress of the fix.

## Our Commitment
- We will acknowledge receipt of your report promptly.
- We will work to provide a fix or mitigation in a timely manner.
- We will provide credit to the reporter in our release notes (unless you prefer to remain anonymous).

Thank you for helping keep magic_profanity secure for everyone.
